WARRIOR MOMENTUM DEFINITION
==========================

This defines WHEN a trade is allowed.
If these are not met → DO NOT TRADE.

--------------------------------------------------
MOMENTUM STATES
--------------------------------------------------

IGNITION
--------
Setup forming, NOT tradable yet.

Conditions:
- Relative volume spike vs premarket baseline
- Price breaks premarket high or key whole/half dollar
- Spread acceptable (not widening)
- Tape speed increasing

IGNITION alone = NO TRADE

--------------------------------------------------
CONFIRMED (REQUIRED FOR ENTRY)
--------------------------------------------------

ALL must be TRUE:

- Holds above VWAP OR clean VWAP reclaim
- Higher highs and higher lows on micro timeframe
- Follow-through within 30–90 seconds
- Volume does NOT collapse after breakout

CONFIRMED = ENTRY ALLOWED

--------------------------------------------------
DECAY
--------------------------------------------------

Momentum weakening.

Triggers:
- Volume fades
- Failed VWAP reclaim
- Lower high after breakout
- Tape slows noticeably

DECAY = EXIT OR NO ENTRY

--------------------------------------------------
DEAD
--------------------------------------------------

Momentum invalid.

Triggers:
- No follow-through
- Liquidity dries up
- Multiple failed attempts

DEAD = FORCED EXIT OR NO_TRADE

--------------------------------------------------
TIME STOPS (NON-NEGOTIABLE)
--------------------------------------------------

- Scalps: 30–60 seconds
- Quick momentum: 2–3 minutes max

No progress = EXIT
Even if position is green.

--------------------------------------------------
STRATEGY DISABLE RULE
--------------------------------------------------

If a strategy has:
- 2 failed CONFIRMED trades
  OR
- >60% veto rate in 10–15 minutes

→ Disable strategy for cooldown (15–30 minutes)

END OF MOMENTUM RULES
